Bugs Bunny is an animated cartoon character.

Created in 1940 by Leon Schlesinger Productions.

Voiced originally by Mel Blanc.

History
72 years ago Bugs Bunny was born on July 27th, 1940. Bugs appeared in Tex Avery's A Wild Hare. The Warner Bros. short is widely considered to be the first definitive Bugs Bunny cartoon, in which the character's appearance, personality and voice gelled as a whole.
