Education

I went to Lincoln Park Co-op preschool, I don't remember much about it other than across the street was a park we would always go to. I would hang out below the doctors office upstairs, and I went to an afterschool program where I made arts and crafts.

From Kindgergarden to 8th grade I attented Hawthorne Academy. A school right next to Wrigley Field with a huge playground and turf field. Me and both my brothers went there, so we have been apart of the community for a while. But I am very happy to say that I graduated because it was getting old.

I now go to Lane Tech, I am a freshman enjoying my time. I play sports, joined a club, and look forward to much more that Lane offers. I plan on staying here all four years as my teachers prepare me for college and my future.

I plan on going to four years of college in the school of my choice. I don't know what that choice is but I think it will be a Big Ten school. I want to make as much money as I possibly can. I dont know what profession interests me, but I know certain ones that dont such as music, art, or literature. I think more modern and popular jobs have more to offer to me.
